About Argentina🇦🇷

Argentina is a countrie in South America thats covers most of the southern portion of the continent. It is the worlds eighth largest country which also occupies an area more extensive than Mexico and the U.S. . It has immense plains, deserts, tundra, and forests, as well as tall mountains, rivers, and thousands of miles of ocean shoreline. Argentina also claims a portion of Antarctica, as well as several islands in the South Atlantic, including the British-ruled Falkland Islands.
